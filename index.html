Intro to MPC

Secure Multiparty Computation (MPC) allows multiple parties to jointly compute a result over their private inputs — without anyone revealing their individual data to the others. In this demo, you will see how three friends can calculate an honest average score while keeping each person's rating completely secret.

The Scenario

Maria is getting married in a week and just got a new hairstyle. She asks her friends Alice, Bob, and Lisa to rate it honestly from 1 to 5. She needs honest opinions, but she is scared that if each friend tells her their opinion directly, they will overestimate their score because of friendship pressure. At the same time, she does not want her friends to discuss her appearance and share their scores with each other. That is why she applies the MPC protocol — to reveal the average score for her hairstyle so that no individual knows the scores of others.
